College library

Shivaji College Library has a diverse collection of over 78,625 (seventy eight thousand six hundred and twenty five) books and 739 CDs of various disciplines.The Library subscribes to 31 newspapers (including multiple copies) and magazines and has a rich collection of various reference sources such as encyclopedias, dictionaries, atlases, and yearbooks, etc.

College Sports Complex

Shivaji College takes pride in maintaining one of the largest sports ground in Delhi University College. All forms of sports such as cricket, football, basketball, table tennis, athletics, softball, and baseball are practised regularly in the sports ground.Facilities like changing rooms and storerooms for students are also available.

College Cafeteria

The canteen committee is responsible for monitoring the operations of the canteen, looking into tenders and coordinating with the vendor. The Shivaji College Canteen committee maintains the highest standards of health and cleanliness and regularly conducts checks regarding hygiene, upgradation and maintenance.
